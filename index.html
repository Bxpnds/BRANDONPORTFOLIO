  • 2009

    My Humble Beginnings

    In September of 2009 I enlisted into the United State Navy. The job I choose was Aviation Structural Mechanic simply because I didn't know anyone that was in the Aviation field. I've always loved the idea of being a game changer.

  • March 2017

    A New Hunger Was Born

    At this point I've been serving in the Navy for eight years and though I was doing well according to Navy standards and excelling in many areas I was unsatisfied. I began to look for career paths that were more promising and rewarding to my family.

  • July 2017-2019

    Transition from boots to suits

    As I began my transition out of the navy I found interest in teaching people how I paid off $20,000 worth of debt in 10 months. That propelled me into being a financial advisor and building an agency. Through my journey I learned that there is a huge problem in our society and it was coming from bad money habits. this is when I began to look for solutions.

  • Jan 2020

    Phase Two

    As my research develops I realize that the answer to most problems are in everyone's pocket. I know what you're thinking money right? Wrong!! Its your cell phone and everything in it. I figured that if I could make an app the help people form better habits then i would leave my mark ojn the world. Thats when a friend called my and told me about Digital Crafts.
